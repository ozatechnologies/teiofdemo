About Us

A distribution company of agro-botanical based natural goods.

Since India is one of the largest spices and natural goods producers in the world, we have a potential opportunity to contribute to the world economy.

TEIOF IMPEX works directly with traditional farmers, applying rigorous international quality standards while preserving the soul of time-honored agricultural methods.

"The Earth Is One Family — every shipment strengthens the bonds of global commerce."
