About TEIOF
International Impex

We prioritize quality and safety at every stage of our operations. Established in 1984, TEIOF Impex Trade Pvt. Ltd. has grown into a trusted name in the global agro-export industry, delivering premium agricultural products to over 50 countries worldwide.

Our ISO 22000:2018, GVCS, and HALAL certifications ensure that our products meet the highest standards of food safety and adhere to the strictest of quality requirements, giving our customers complete peace of mind.

Further, our USFDA, FFRMS, REX, and GMP registrations underscore our commitment to delivering products that meet international standards — backed by four decades of expertise and a passion for agricultural excellence.

Supply Chain Management Process
